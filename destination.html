Jaipur – The Pink City

Palaces, forts, and royal heritage – discover Rajasthan’s crown jewel.

Quick Information

📍 Location

Rajasthan, North India

🕒 Best Time to Visit

October to March

🚆 How to Reach

✈️ Jaipur International Airport (12 km from city)
🚆 Jaipur Junction Railway Station
🚗 Well connected by road (NH8 from Delhi)

History & Significance

Jaipur, founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, is famously known as the “Pink City” because of the distinct color of its buildings. It is a UNESCO World Heritage City, celebrated for its grand forts, royal palaces, vibrant bazaars, and rich culture. Jaipur forms part of India’s Golden Triangle (Delhi–Agra–Jaipur) and is one of the most visited destinations in India.

Things to Do

🏰 Amber Fort

Explore the majestic fort with stunning architecture and elephant rides.

🌸 Hawa Mahal

Iconic “Palace of Winds” with unique honeycomb structure.

🛍️ Johari Bazaar

Shop for jewelry, handicrafts, and traditional Rajasthani textiles.

🌅 Nahargarh Fort

Enjoy panoramic sunset views of Jaipur city.

Travel Tips

  • Carry light cotton clothes during summers, warm clothes in winters.
  • Bargain while shopping in local bazaars.
  • Try Rajasthani cuisine: Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar, and Laal Maas.
  • Hire a local guide for detailed history and hidden gems.
